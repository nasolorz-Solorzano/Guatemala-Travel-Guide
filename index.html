Welcome to Guatemala

Guatemala is a country full of culture, history, and natural beauty. From ancient Mayan ruins to volcanoes and colorful markets, there is something for every traveler.

This website is designed to introduce visitors to some of Guatemala’s most popular destinations while encouraging respectful and meaningful travel.

Popular Destinations

Tikal

Ancient Mayan ruins located in the rainforest and one of Guatemala’s most famous historical sites.

Antigua

A historic city known for its colonial architecture, colorful streets, and nearby volcano views.

Lake Atitlán

A beautiful lake surrounded by volcanoes and villages that offer culture, nature, and relaxation.
